About

I started in 2016 testing software by hand, and I've spent the years since moving steadily closer to the root of the problem — from finding bugs, to automating the search for them, to building the platforms and now the AI agents that keep an entire engineering org moving. The thread is constant: I'd rather build the tool that solves a problem a hundred times than solve it once.

Focused now on   autonomous AI tooling, MCP servers, and the developer-experience platform that the rest of engineering builds on.
